Tafesse Muluneh is a prolific author. His love of books began in childhood and evolved into a lifelong passion. To date, Tafesse has released a number of books to the publishing market in Ethiopia — and beyond! His books span a wide variety of topics: from science to religion, to technology and sports. This page is dedicated to honoring Tafesse's lifelong achievements and his contribution to his country through publications. The books engage readers to gain knowledge and promote curiosity about various subjects in accessible ways. It is the author's mission to inspire the next generation to follow in his footsteps and become empowered and productive through reading and knowledge sharing.

Tafesse's publications at a glance

ሳይንቲስቶች

Scientists (Amharic)

Download eBook

The Scientists book presents the work of modern-era scientists. Countless books have been published about "old-day" scientists like Galileo Galilei, Sir Isaac Newton, and others, but the 21st-century generation must also know about and appreciate today's researchers on the forefront of science who have shaped modern science and technology. This book will help Ethiopian youth understand recent scientific discoveries.

የ ሃይማኖቶች ልዪነት

Differences Among Religions (Amharic)

Download eBook

This masterpiece is one of the most popular publications from Tafesse. It is a curated list of some of the major religions being practiced around the world. The topics within explore the similarities and variations among the religions in an effort to help readers understand the basics of religious dogma. Readers will find it interesting as it will help them put their believes in contrast to most other religions and thereby start asking critical questions.

Nuclear Energy (Amharic)

Download eBook

The first of its kind on the shelves of Ethiopian book stores, this book takes the reader through the origin, development, and advancement of nuclear energy, placing greater emphasis on the overall effects of nuclear research in relation to its advantages and disadvantages. It showcases scientific areas that benefited from the culmination of nuclear science, as well as its environmental dangers and the politics involved.

ሌሎች ዓለማት

Different Worlds (Amharic)

Download eBook

This book marks Tafesse's very first publication. In his college years, Tafesse studied Astrophysics both at home and abroad. At the time, space research was in its infancy in Ethiopia — largely unknown prior to the establishment of the Smithsonian research facility in Debre Zeyt. After graduation, Tafesse taught Physics at Addis Ababa University, a prestigious campus in Ethiopia at the time. He was troubled to see that all reference materials and terminology were in foreign languages. Tafesse worked to change that, which laid the groundwork for writing this book in 1986.

የ ቼዝ ጥበብ

Mastering Chess (Amharic)

Download eBook

"The one and only sport match in the entire world that is 100% mind-only is Chess", these are Taf's own words when he describes the book to me. The inspiration behind the writing of this handbook came while Tafesse was the President of the Ethiopian Chess Federation. The game wasn't so popular in Ethiopia whilst its benefits to youth in mind development, especially in problem solving and personal discipline are so obvious. Distributing Chess boards to schools and amateur clubs alone won't do the job unless prospective players are supplied with a supporting guide. This brochure will entice and indulge players by studying power moves taken by world renowned Chess players and thereby helping players analyze and build on their skills. The book can serve as a solid guide for chess players and prospective fans looking to sharpen their skills at the board game.
