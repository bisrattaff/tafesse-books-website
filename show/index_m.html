Tafesse Muluneh is a prolific author. His love of books began in childhood and evolved into becoming a lifelong hobby. To present, Tafesse has released a number of books to the publishing market in Ethiopia - and beyond! His books span a wide variety of topics: from science to religion, to technology and sports too! This page is dedicated to honor Tafesse's life long achievements and his contribution to his country thru publications. The books engage readers to gain knowledge and promote curiosity on various subject matters in simplistic ways. It is the utmost mission of the author to entice the future generation to follow similar footsteps to become empowered and productive thru reading and knowledge sharing.

“Tafesse is a brilliant writer with six books under his belt. He knows not only what to write but how to write it. I admire that all his writings are lean and to the point. Scientists (Amharic)

Download eBook

Scientists book presents the work of the modern era scientists.Countless number of books have been published about the "old-day" scientists like Galileo Galilei, Sir. Issac Newton, ... and so on, but the 21st century generation must also know about and appreciate the present day researchers on the forefront of science who spearheaded modern science and technology to its present shape. This book will help fellow Ethiopian youth in understanding the advancement of recent scientific discoveries.

Differences Among Religions (Amharic)

Download eBook

This masterpiece is one of the most popular publications from Tafesse. It is a curated list of some of the major religions being practiced around the world. The topics within explore the similarities and variations among the religions in an effort to help readers understand the basics of religious dogma. Readers will find it interesting as it will help them put their believes in contrast to most other religions and thereby start asking critical questions.

Nuclear Energy (Amharic)

The first of it's kind on the shelves of Ethiopian book stores, this book takes the reader thru the origin, development and advancement of the nuclear energy, putting greater emphasis on the overall effect of nuclear research and advancement in relation to its advantages and disadvantages. It showcases scientific areas that benefited from culmination of nuclear science, as well as its environmental dangers imposed by nations and the politics involved.

ሌሎች ዓለማት

Different Worlds (Amharic)

Download eBook

This book marks the very first publication for Tafesse. In his college years Tafesse studied Astrophysics, both home and abroad. At the time, space research was in its infancy in Ethiopias3 unknown prior to the advent of the Smithsonian research facility in Debrezeyt. Post graduation, Tafesse taught Physics courses at Addis Ababa University, a prestige campus in Ethiopia at the time. He wasn't delighted to see that all reference materials and terminologies were in foreign language. Tafesse worked to change that and that laid the ground to the writing of this piece in 1986.

የ ቼዝ ጥበብ

Mastering Chess (Amharic)

Download eBook

"The one and only sport match in the entire world that is 100% mind-only is Chess", these are Taf's own words when he describes the book to me. The inspiration behind the writing of this handbook came while Tafesse was the President of the Ethiopian Chess Federation. The game wasn't so popular in Ethiopia whilst its benefits to youth in mind development, especially in problem solving and personal discipline are so obvious. Distributing Chess boards to schools and amateur clubs alone won't do the job unless prospective players are supplied with a supporting guide. This brochure will entice and indulge players by studying power moves taken by world renowned Chess players and thereby helping players analyze and build on their skills. The book can serve as a solid guide for chess gamer and prospective fans who are looking forward to sharpening their skills on the board game.
